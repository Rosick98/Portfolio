The Travel Buddy. Good company is always the best choice

The Problem.

In 2024, global travel surged by 17%, yet Airbnb's revenue dropped 15%. Travelers now prioritize immersive experiences over just accommodations, revealing gaps in Airbnb’s model. Without integrated local guidance or flexible trip planning, users seek alternatives that offer deeper cultural connections, forcing Airbnb to rethink its approach.

The Solution

This new Airbnb extension will help the company attract a new user base. Our goal is to connect this new era of travelers with friendly locals, making travel more fun and help bring more revenue for the company

Features & Funtionalities

  • Questionnaire to provide user with personalized results.
  • Direct message with your Travel Buddy prior to booking
  • Flexible booking options and group size
  • Maintaining app consistency to make sure existing users can easily navigate the extension

Competitor Analysis

Trip Advisor

TripAdvisor offers travel reviews and booking, while this Airbnb extension connects travelers with local chaperons for personalized planning, flexible itineraries, and authentic experiences.

Expedia

Expedia simplifies travel booking with flights, hotels, and vacation packages, while this Airbnb extension enhances exploration by pairing travelers with local guides who provide customized trip planning, adaptable itineraries, and immersive cultural experiences.

Future Ideations

  • AI-Powered Personalized Itineraries – Users receive tailored travel plans based on preferences, previous trips, and local recommendations.
  • Live Translation & Cultural Guidance – Built-in real-time translation and etiquette tips to ease communication and cultural immersion.
  • Local Safety Alerts & Assistance – Instant notifications about local risks, safe zones, and emergency contacts specific to each destination.
  • Group Travel Coordination – A feature that helps friends or families plan trips together with synced itineraries and shared bookings.
